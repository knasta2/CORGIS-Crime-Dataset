Effects of Roe v Wade on crime in the U.S.

In one of his works, a famous American Economist Steven Levitt argues that one of the unexpected side effects of legalizing abortions in all of the U.S. was a noticable decrease in crime 15-20 years later. The economist makes a connection that unwanted children have been shown at high risk for crime, so less unwanted children could mean less crime 15-20 year into the future. The economist says that the data supports this hypothesis, however none of the sources show any data that supports this claim. Using this dataset we will either confirm or disprove this hypothesis.
